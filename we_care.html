Our Community

The heart of Lube Town is Okotoks. We are thankful to be able to give back to a community that have so much to offer. Lube Town is proud supporter of:

  • Okotoks EMS
  • Okotoks Fire Fighters
  • Okotoks Police
  • Foothills Composite High School
  • Okotoks Rotary Club
  • Chamber of commerce
  • Okotoks Agricultural Society

Beyond our community

  • Canadian Breast Cancer Foundation
  • Wild Rose Community Connections
  • Alberta Fire Chief Association
  • Canadian Fallen Fire Fighters
  • Alberta Cancer Foundation
  • Ducks Unlimited Canada
  • Ride to Conquer Cancer

Globally

Pioneers –from local to global, our world and beyond, providing food, education and opportunity for children in developing countries.

Lube town get & give program (5 + 5)

WIN-WIN community based marketing program. The customer gets $5 off their oil change and Lube Town in turn donates another $5 to community programs. Lube Town never turns any one down, with community based donations of over $16,000. If you are a community group that is interested in participating in this program, please contact us.

Community support/volunteer:

  • Ducks unlimited, Canadian Breast Cancer Foundation, Pioneers, Okotoks fire department
  • Feed the Hungry –providing hot meals and a warm and welcoming experience for the needy
  • Foundation for the Future Charter Academy – Assist teachers in providing a rewarding learning experience for elementary children
  • Parkdale Community Association – Casino
  • Calgary South Rotary Club
  • Casino – Raising funds for the Calgary South Rotary
  • Stampede Dream Home – Calgary stampede fundraiser proceeds go to local charities throughout the community. Thanks to Calgary South Rotary, funds raised exceeds $400,000.
  • Co-chaired Calgary South Rotary fundraiser, over $33,000 raised in April
  • Fort McMurray -$2 of donated for every oil change done in May
